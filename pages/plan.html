Strategic Action Plan

Mr. Nooka Reddy Garu's plan of action for the brighter future in inevitable. His vision is to uplift the society and the nation with his strategic action plan. The plan includes various aspects such as rural development, education, health, and more.

Education

  • Advocacy for Infrastructure: Push for state funding for new school buildings, classrooms, libraries, laboratories, and sanitation facilities in underserved areas.
  • Quality of Education: Advocate for improved teacher training programs, recruitment of qualified educators, and provision of modern teaching aids and technology (e.g., smart classrooms, computer labs).
  • Scholarships and Financial Aid: Promote awareness and facilitate access to state-sponsored scholarships, stipends, and financial aid schemes for students from economically weaker sections.
  • Skill Development Integration: Lobby for the inclusion of vocational training and skill development programs in school and college curricula to enhance employability.
  • Higher Education Access: Advocate for the establishment of more colleges, universities, or specialized institutions within the district, or for increased intake capacity in existing ones.

Employment

  • Skill Development Centers: Advocate for the establishment and expansion of government-run skill development centers providing training in in-demand trades (e.g., IT, electronics, tailoring, hospitality, agriculture).
  • Promoting Local Industries: Support policies and provide incentives that attract investment in local industries, thereby creating job opportunities.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Facilitate access to state government schemes for young entrepreneurs, including mentorship, seed funding, and simplified business registration.
  • Job Fairs and Placements: Organize or facilitate government-sponsored job fairs in collaboration with local businesses and industries to connect job seekers with employers.
  • Data-Driven Employment Initiatives: Push for surveys to identify specific skill gaps in the district and advocate for tailored training programs to address them.

Healthcare & Public Services

  • Healthcare Access & Infrastructure: Advocate for upgrading district and primary health centers, increasing bed capacity, ensuring availability of essential medicines, and recruiting doctors and paramedics.
  • Sanitation & Water Supply: Push for improved sanitation infrastructure, waste management systems, and access to clean drinking water (e.g., borewells, water treatment plants, piped water schemes).
  • Roads & Connectivity: Lobby for the construction and maintenance of district roads, connectivity to remote villages, and improved public transportation.
  • Electricity Supply: Advocate for consistent and quality electricity supply, especially in rural and industrial areas.
  • Public Distribution System (PDS) Monitoring: Conduct oversight to ensure the efficient and corruption-free functioning of PDS, ensuring essential commodities reach eligible beneficiaries.

Social Wellfare & Community Development

  • Awareness & Access to Schemes: Organize public awareness campaigns and workshops to inform people about various state and central government welfare schemes (e.g., pensions, housing, food security, women and child development programs) and assist in application processes.
  • Women's Empowerment: Support initiatives for women's self-help groups (SHGs), promote financial literacy, and advocate for safety measures and gender equality.
  • Farmers' Welfare: Advocate for timely disbursement of crop loans, crop insurance, fair prices for agricultural produce, and access to modern farming techniques and irrigation facilities.
  • Senior Citizen & Differently-abled Support: Push for better facilities and more robust support systems for senior citizens and persons with disabilities.
  • Sports & Culture: Advocate for the development of local sports facilities, promotion of regional arts and culture, and organization of community events.

Governance & Accountability

  • Representing Grievances: Serve as a direct channel for people to voice their grievances and concerns to the state government and relevant departments.
  • Budgetary Allocation: Advocate for a fair share of the state budget to be allocated for district-specific development projects.
  • Policy Feedback: Gather feedback from the ground level to inform state policy decisions and ensure they are tailored to local needs.
  • Transparency & Anti-Corruption: Actively participate in oversight committees and public forums to ensure transparency in government projects and advocate for anti-corruption measures.
